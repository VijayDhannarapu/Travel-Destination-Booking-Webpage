Places

Coorg, often called the Scotland of India, is a beautiful hill station in Karnataka known for its lush coffee plantations, misty landscapes, and rich culture. It's a perfect getaway for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and those looking to relax in the peaceful beauty of the Western Ghats.

Dhanaulti is a quiet hill station in Uttarakhand, located near Mussoorie, known for its serene environment, dense forests, and panoramic Himalayan views. Far from the crowds, it's a peaceful retreat with adventure activities, eco-parks, and scenic beauty.

Puducherry, often called Pondicherry, is a charming coastal town on the southeastern coast of India, known for its French colonial architecture, serene beaches, and spiritual retreats. The mix of French and Indian culture makes it a unique and peaceful travel destination.

Rishikesh is a scenic town in Uttarakhand, India, situated on the banks of the Ganges River. Known as the “Yoga Capital of the World,” it is famous for its spiritual retreats, yoga centers, historic temples, and adventure activities like white-water rafting. The blend of natural beauty and spiritual culture makes it a serene and inspiring destination.
